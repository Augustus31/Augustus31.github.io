Special Characters App

It's now much easier to type special characters in Spanish and Portuguese.

There's no more need to deal with cumbersome alt keys, copy-pasting, or changing keyboards. Easy Language Keyboards make it easy to type any special character in just seconds. Download it here.

But why not just use a foreign language keyboard?

Because it's easier to just use this app. Here's why:

  1. No need to press shift or otherwise break the flow of your typing.
  2. No dead keys.
  3. Letter first, modifier second. This is more intutitive than the reverse, which is the case in most foreign language keyboard setups.
  4. Easy activation and deactivation, with one click.
